# All-in-one-NEWS
This Android app displays all big NEWS channel`s news in one place.TIMES OF INDIA , THE HUNDASTHAN TIMES ,
BBC NEWS ,GOOGLE NEWS , NET. GEO. NEWS ,MTV NEWS CNN NEWS, CNBC NEWS BBC SPORTS , TIME , ESPN NEWS etc.
